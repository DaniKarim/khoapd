Project Overview
The Pepsi Karaoke mobile web app enables users to express their passion for music by recording and sharing their performances of trending songs. With a simple and intuitive interface, users can easily join the karaoke experience and become part of a community of music enthusiasts. The top 3 most loved karaoke performances will receive exciting prizes.
Project Goal
  • Increase brand awareness for Pepsi through a fun, interactive, and easily accessible musical experience tailored for the young audience.
  • Create an online music playground where users can showcase their singing talents with trending songs and effortlessly share their recordings.
  • Build a community of music lovers who engage with the brand through voting, sharing, and social interactions.
  • Attract new users and boost engagement by incorporating a competitive element - voting and rewarding the most popular performances.
  • Gather user behavior data to support future Pepsi marketing campaigns and audience insights.

Infor
Montserrat is a typeface designed by Julieta Ulanovsky, inspired by old posters and signs from the Montserrat neighborhood in Buenos Aires. The project aims to preserve and revive the beauty of urban typography from the first half of the 20th century, which is gradually disappearing due to urban development. Released under the SIL Open Font License, Montserrat is free to use. In addition to the regular Montserrat family, there are two sister families: Alternates (featuring unique letterforms) and Subrayada (meaning “underlined” in Spanish, with integrated underline elements in the letters). In 2017, the font was redrawn by Jacques Le Bailly to improve its readability for longer texts. The team also expanded support for Cyrillic script, with contributions from several type experts.
