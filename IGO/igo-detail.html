Project Overview
What is Igo Travel Booking App? This is an application that helps you book rooms for outings, whether at home or abroad. In addition, you can search for resort tours from casual to high-end. Finally, the community function of IGO allows you to share with people beautiful images of the places you have been to, thereby building a travel community and connecting people on the IGO app.
Research Process
Before starting development processi conducted in depth research.
What are the gathered statistics?
  • IGO users are in the age group of 20-35 This is the age group that has a job anda stable income willing to experience new things, connect with people. In addition, this is the age group that is develop their career, define themselves, get married and above all define the age of 30 from society and they are facing a lot of pressure from many sides.
  • Statistics from the Institute of Mental Health - Bach Mai Hospital show that in Vietnam, about 30% of the population suffers from mental disorders, of which the rate of depression accounts for 25%.Every year, the number of people working in treating depression in our country is worth from 36,000 to 40,000 people. and now at a rate the rate is being rejuvenated.
  • Therefore, the need to relieve pressure through trips is absolutely necessary.
What is the user's goal?
  • Want to use time and budget effectively for trips from scheduling of planning to the end of the trip.
What is the value provided to users?
For user
  • There is a community with the same interest in travel. Have more knowledge, experiences in places on the world.
  • Maximum saving time for searching for places, planning for trips.
  • Create connections between peozple, save the memories for user.
  • Searching your place easily, pay easily when booking.
For IGO
  • The community explore feature allows users to share photos, helping more people know about IGO.
  • Business with more partners in tourist destinations such as restaurants, hotels, resorts in tourist destinations.
  • When there is an ideal numbers of user, business business can increase sales from advertising for place, show and festivals are about to take place throught IGOâ€™s Travel blogs feature.
What problems might arise when starting a trip?
Based on the survey of Vietnamese travel experience behavior in 2018, it shows that a large numberof people prefer to travel independently (78%) and only (28%) choose to travel by tour
To prepare for the trip, usually they have to make a plan or simply follow the inspiration, but often encounter problems such as:
  • It take too much time for find trip information.
  • They have difficulty determinin travel time and distance from point to point to arrange the schedule so that it is reasonable.
  • There is no place to connect travel enthusiasts with each other.
What are the solutions to these issues?
From the problems that users encounter and the application offers the following solution!
  • To solve this problem, the IGO application has provided a solution for users with more detailed choices. Users only need to select a destination IGO will suggest to users hotels and tours in the destination selected by the user with full information (prices, schedules, services, ...).
  • Propose a suggested schedule based on the information, internets that users have provided, or based on the highlights of the same direction.
  • Besides booking and tours. IGO has built a travel community when users of the application, users can post moments, videos, photos to the IGO community.
