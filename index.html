About me

Hi, I’m Khoa Pham, a UX/UI designer based in Ho Chi Minh City. With a passion for user experience design, I believe that attention to detail in design can enhance the user experience.

Currently, I'm working in Ho Chi Minh City as a UX/UI designer. I'm always open to connecting with people, so if you're interested in collaborating, feel free to reach out to me.

My goal is to connect with people, understand their problems, and solve them through thoughtful designs that deliver satisfying experiences while also benefiting the business.
