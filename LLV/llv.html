Project Overview
Lac Loc Vang was a nationwide loyalty campaign initiated by Vinh Tuong, targeting its network of construction contractors across Vietnam. Taking place from November 1, 2021 to January 20, 2022, the campaign welcomed participation from all eligible partners. It aimed to strengthen long-term relationships and express gratitude for the continued trust and support of the contractor community. Participants had the opportunity to win high-value rewards, including 64 savings certificates worth up to 15 billion VND in total, 100 PNJ gold charms, and thousands of practical gifts such as 3,000 sets of adhesive tapes and screws. More than just a prize program, the campaign reinforced Vinh Tuongâ€™s ongoing commitment to its partners across the construction industry.

Mascot for Lac Loc Vang
This cheerful mascot blends the image of a construction worker and the Vietnamese Fortune God. His playful poses reflect luck, joy, and the spirit of the Lac Loc Vang campaign
