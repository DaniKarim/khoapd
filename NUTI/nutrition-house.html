Project Overview
Nutrition house is an online store on device platforms such as desktop and mobile that specializes in selling Nutifood products developed based on virtual reality technology (VR 360), giving users a shopping experience. Choose authentic products like at the store.
Project Goal
Immersive Shopping Experience
The goal of "Nutrition House Store (VR360)" is to create a highly immersive online shopping experience through the use of VR (360) technology. This allows users to browse and select Nutifood products in a virtual environment that simulates the look and feel of a real store.
Cross-Platform Availability
Nutrition House will be accessible on both desktop and mobile devices, ensuring that users can enjoy the experience regardless of the platform they prefer.
Authentic Product Presentation
Through VR (360), products will be showcased in a realistic manner, providing users with detailed views and allowing them to feel more confident in their purchasing decisions, as if they were shopping in a physical store.
Improve Customer Engagement
The virtual shopping experience aims to engage customers on a deeper level, making the shopping process more enjoyable and interactive, thereby increasing customer satisfaction and retention.
