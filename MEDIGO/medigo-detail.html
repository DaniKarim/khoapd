Overview
Medigo Software is a healthcare technology company that provides online pharmacy services. The app connects patients with nearby pharmacies, allowing them to order medications and receive home delivery.
Medigo offers features such as pharmacist consultation, prescription management, and a wide range of medications. The app aims to improve access to healthcare, especially for those who need fast and reliable medication delivery services.
