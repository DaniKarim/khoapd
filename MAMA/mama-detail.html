Project Overview
MAMA is an application for students to use to manage courses, study sessions and store learned content. In addition, the application has some essential features for the learning process
Project Goal
Students can manage their own learning path and keep track of the upcoming class schedule
  • Manage the course: see the name of the teacher, the timetable, the duration of the course (number of hours studied, number of hours not studied), the status of the course.
  • Manage resources: learning materials, teaching videos, exercises.
  • Manage learning results: tests, test results.
  • Update information: change class time, change teacher.
Students can report their absence and change their schedule
  • Allow students to report absences or change class schedules directly on the app.
  • Notify teachers and class administrators when there is a request to report absence or schedule changes.
  • Update information: class time after change.
Confirm the lesson /class time
  • Study time management: students can confirm whether the session has been completed or not.
  • Update information: update the status of the session, the duration of the course.
Review of the session
  • Review to evaluate the quality of each session.

UX Research
For my research plan, I first defined my target audience, which consists of students enrolled at the center. Then, I proceeded to identify the difficulties students face during the learning process. I found that more than 60% of our students want to manage their study time, materials, and course content, 30% of students think it is okay but not a priority, and 10% of students are indifferent to this matter.
Problem Finding & Solution
Problems
  • It is difficult for students to keep track of courses such as class time, class schedule, course duration, ...
  • Students forget to come to class.
  • Students can't manage the content they have learned, exercise files, learning materials.
  • Requests to absence , change class schedules must contact the class manager themselves (it takes time, class managers may forget to process student requests).
  • Students have no control over whether the lesson actually takes place.
Solutions
  • Display detailed course information including: Teacher's name, course duration, class schedule, study topic, content, exercise file, learning materials of each session when the teacher updates.
  • Notice to remind students before class on the app.
  • Submit an application for leave or reschedule directly on the application, the request will be notified via email or notified on the system for class administrator and notified via email or on the application for teachers.
  • Students have the right to confirm session directly on the application whether the lesson really took place or not.

Infor
Montserrat is a typeface designed by Julieta Ulanovsky, inspired by old posters and signs from the Montserrat neighborhood in Buenos Aires. The project aims to preserve and revive the beauty of urban typography from the first half of the 20th century, which is gradually disappearing due to urban development. Released under the SIL Open Font License, Montserrat is free to use. In addition to the regular Montserrat family, there are two sister families: Alternates (featuring unique letterforms) and Subrayada (meaning “underlined” in Spanish, with integrated underline elements in the letters). In 2017, the font was redrawn by Jacques Le Bailly to improve its readability for longer texts. The team also expanded support for Cyrillic script, with contributions from several type experts.

Visual Design
Here are some of the final design screens that we share
Sign In - Forgot Password
After downloading the application, students can log in using the email or phone number they used during registration. If they forget their password, they can click the "Forgot Password" link and enter their registered email or phone number to reset it.
Learning schedule - My Courses
After logging in, the main screen will display your weekly class schedule, making it easy to track and plan your participation. In the course section, you can conveniently manage all courses, including their status, duration, and each student's learning progress.
Find a Teacher
Students can search for teachers here. The platform displays teacher profiles of those currently teaching at the center, including their class schedules, ratings, and more. Students can also book trial lessons directly from this section.
Leave of Absence
This feature allows students to notify both the center and their teacher in advance when they are unable to attend a class. Students can select the specific class, provide a reason, and submit their absence request directly through the app.
